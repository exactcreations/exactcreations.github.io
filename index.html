About Exact Creations

We are new...We are Fresh....Yet !! Creative and Energetic.

Exact Creations is a design, animation and development company located in Lucknow, India. We have a small core team, but a large minded of creative members.

We are very passionate about design, characters and innovation. And even more so we are passionate about combining all of these elements in high quality productions that are as engaging, exciting and entertaining for the clients as they are for us to create.

Exact Creations aspire to bring a unique blend of imagination, talent and creativity to the worlds of animation and development company across the world. We believe that a compelling piece of design and story telling is the product of constantly evolving a concept through effective collabration and communication with our clients and each other. Our process is fresh and fluid every project is different with diverse and contrasting needs and we taylor our approach to each project individually.

If you are interested in what we do or just want to hear more, please don't hesitate to Contact Us.
